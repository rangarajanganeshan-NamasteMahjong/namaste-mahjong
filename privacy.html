Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Namaste Mahjong ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is a 2D Mahjong Solitaire puzzle game designed for entertainment purposes.

1. What Information Does the Application Obtain?

User Provided Information: The Application does not require any registration, account creation, or user profile setup. We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.

Automatically Collected Information: The Application may collect certain information automatically to improve game performance, including the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, and your mobile operating system.

2. Real-Time Location Information

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

3. Third-Party Access and Advertising

We do not share any data with third parties. If our game utilizes standard mobile ad networks (such as Google AdMob) or platform analytics (such as Apple App Analytics or Google Play Analytics), those services may collect non-personal data according to their respective privacy policies to serve relevant ads or provide crash reports.

4. Data Retention Policy

Since we do not collect or store user-provided personal data, we do not retain or manage any personal data databases.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via the developer communication channels provided on the App Store or Google Play Store.
